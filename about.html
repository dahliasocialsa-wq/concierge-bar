Our story

The detail is
everything.

Concierge Bar started with a simple frustration: the best gifts — the ones that actually feel considered — are surprisingly hard to give. Not because the right things don't exist. They do. They're in small shops on quiet streets in Lisbon, in markets that only locals know, with makers who don't ship to your door.

We built Concierge Bar to close that gap. To be the person who knows where to look, who has the relationships, and who will put something together that couldn't have come from anywhere else.

We're based in Lisbon, which means we work with the city rather than around it. The Portuguese craft scene is extraordinary. The food and wine culture is underappreciated. We find the right things, curate them properly, and ship them across Europe.

For those who live in Lisbon and need more than just a gift box, we also offer personal shopping and lifestyle management — the kind of support that makes the city feel like it's working with you rather than against you.

What we believe

How we think
about our work.

01
Curation over selection

There's a difference between choosing from a list and curating for a person. We do the latter — which means sometimes saying no to something that doesn't quite fit.

02
Local knowledge matters

We're in Lisbon. We know the city's makers, its markets, its neighbourhoods. That knowledge is the product — not just a nice backstory.

03
No shortcuts

Every box is packed by hand. Every curation is made to order. We don't hold stock or fill gaps with generic items. If it doesn't earn its place, it doesn't go in.
